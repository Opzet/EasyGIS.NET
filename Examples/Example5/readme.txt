Example 5

This example shows how to display a marker centered on a map loaded in the EGIS.Controls.SFMap Control.
The example loads a collection of logged GPS data packets and iterates over the GPS data, displaying a marker at the  current location.
    
- To pan around the map, click and drag the left mouse button on the map.

- To zoom into the map double click the left mouse button or use the mouse wheel.

- To zoom out of the map double click the right mouse button or use the mouse wheel.
